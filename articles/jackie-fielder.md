Jackie Fielder is an American politician serving as the District 9 Supervisor
on the San Francisco Board of Supervisors.[^wd]

Fielder was elected in November 2024 via ranked-choice voting, succeeding
Hillary Ronen to represent the Mission District, Bernal Heights, and Portola
neighborhoods. She took office on January 8, 2025.[^election]
